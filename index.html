    Tortilla

    Tortilla de patatas is a classic Spanish dish that has been enjoyed for generations. It is a delicious and comforting dish that can be customized with additional ingredients such as cheese, ham, or peppers. It is also a great dish for using up leftover ingredients and is a popular choice for picnics and gatherings due to its versatility and ease of transport.

    Fruit Salad

    Fruit salad is a popular dish made from a combination of fresh fruit that is typically cut into bite-sized pieces and mixed together in a bowl or container. Fruit salad is a nutritious and delicious way to enjoy a variety of fruits, especially during the warmer months when many fruits are in season.

    Muthokoi

    Muthokoi is a popular dish in Kenya, especially among the Kamba ethnic group who traditionally farm maize, one of the key ingredients in this dish. Muthokoi is a hearty and nutritious dish made from boiled and mashed maize kernels, also known as corn kernels, mixed with beans and flavored with various spices and herbs.
